More About Me

Hey there!

I was born and raised in Southern California. I’m also a happily married wife and dog/cat mom of our jack russel terrier, dachshund, and tabby cat. In our free time, we’re usually talking to, singing off-key to and playing with one of these guys.

After years of working administrative roles, and several customer service jobs, I became highly interested in the technologies used to build the internet. As a person who enjoys problem solving, the fact that these technologies are available for anyone to learn was just the challenge I was seeking. I dove right in to teach myself the basics of web design. With the help of various online resources, With just those skills, I managed to get a few clients and a job as web content manager for a digital agency.

As time went on I decided that I wanted to become a web developer, and needed to bring more structure into my learning. I’m currently studying Lambda’s Full Stack Web Development course. I look forward to being challenged to the max during this time, and obtaining the necessary skills for a position as a web developer.

…Meanwhile, I’m always looking to add to my portfolio or contribute to a team in a web development environment! Please review my resume and contact me if you are interested in working together.

Have a great day!
